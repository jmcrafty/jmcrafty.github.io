    Semiconductor Failure Analysis Products

    RKD Engineering designs, manufactures, and sells an extensive line of failure analysis and sample preparation products. These products allow an analyst to open a wide range of integrated circuit packages with various synthetic polymers and internal metals.

    Our products expose the die surface and bond wires, and can be used to precavitate IC packages. The process is completed in a safe and efficient manner using an automated fluid pump.

    RKD Engineering continues to develop products that will make sample preparation easier, faster, and more accurate.

    RKD Engineering

    RKD Engineering specializes in acid decapsulation for semiconductor packages. With over 50 years of experience in the industry, our mission is to provide the highest quality products for semiconductor failure analysis by using a patented "wet etch" acid delivery system.

    Product Features:
    • The most complete line of Failure Analysis sample preparation systems.
    • Easy to use counterfeit detection systems and equipment packages.
    • Integrated chemical resistant fluid handling systems.

    Counterfeit Detection

    Our products can be used to understand your supplier better. By using jet etch decapsulation, your enterprise can ensure that the semiconductor products you pay for are the products you receive.

    Considerations:
    • Are the best quality materials used in the package?
    • Does the manufacturer identifier appear on the die?
    • Has the semiconductor been stored or shipped safely?

    Fluid Handling

    RKD Engineering Corp, Inc. designs and manufactures positive displacement diaphragm metering pumps and fluid handling systems. All our pumps have PTFE (Teflon) wetted parts and are pneumatically actuated to totally isolate the fluid path to the package.

    These are used in our Semiconductor Failure Analysis products pumping very corrosive fuming nitric and sulfuric acids as well as organic solvents.
