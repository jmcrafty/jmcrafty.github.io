    What does RKD Engineering do?

    Top

    RKD Engineering manufactures and distributes equipment for the semiconductor industry. Our decapsulator products are used to prepare microchips for failure analysis. Our products are intended for semiconductor manufacturers, IC counterfeit detection, and research institutions. We also provide precision fluid handling pumps for research and development projects and prototyping. RKD continuously innovates new products and designs industry applications.


    How does the product work?

    Top

    Our decapsulator is designed to accurately and efficiently dissolve encapsulant materials to reveal the silicon die and bond wires in any IC package. Using an automated system, the process can be completed in minutes without causing any damage to the silicon and metal structures. This gives the researcher the ability to run the chip through failure analysis testing procedures.


    How large is the product?

    Top

    The decapsulator easily fits in any standard fume hood and is light enough for the operator to position by hand. An acid bottle holding box sits adjacent to the etcher system for ease of access and replacement.


    Why should I buy your product?

    Top

    RKD Engineering has 5 generations of tooling experience in decapsulation. Our products are machined and built in-house, and come with a long life expectancy and an extensive warranty.
