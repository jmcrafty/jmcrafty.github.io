    Mega Etch 7300

    The Mega Etch 7300 was designed with the PCB manufacturer in mind. The ability to open IC packages which are still attached to the original PC board allows the failure analysis engineer to power the board even after an operation has been performed. The Mega Etch includes all functionality of other RKD Engineering decapsulator models.

    Board Level Acid Decapsulation System - with ESD

    Mega Etch is an Automated Mixed Acid Decapsulator with advanced feature integration to enable high productivity. This Decapsulator easily and rapidly opens even the most delicate COB packages by delivering precise, micro-aliquots of nitric, sulfuric, or acid mixes to the package with no sample damage. The delivery of each micro-aliquot is done with suffcient pressure to create extreme turbulence in the etched cavity that greatly accelerates the rate of encapsulant removal.

    Very low, precise acid temperatures, combined with high micro-aliquot delivery rates allow for the decapsulation of copper wired devices with no wire or metallization damage. An exclusive acid delivery function can be selected that delivers the highest pulse rate possible while consuming less than maximum acid volumes. The specially designed acid heat exchanger can accurately control acid temperature down to 10°C and up to 250°C, with Ô¨Çow rates to 8 ml. per minute using pulse mode. Such high acid pulse rates achieve reasonable etch times even at the lowest temperatures.

    The Mega Etch operates at temperatures up to 250°C enabling operational versatility with any combination of acids. The monolithic etch head is machined from premium grade silicon carbide for unsurpassed acid resistance. The etch head is designed to reduce the fuming of any residual acids left on the etch head at the end of the process.

    The device hold down assembly (ram nose) is pneumatically activated and is designed for a large amount of vertical travel. The ram nose is normally retracted and only extends after the safety cover is fully closed. The vertical movement of the ram nose secures the device to the etch head thus eliminating movement of either the package or the board fxturing. RKD Engineering is the only company to incorporate true double containment for all Ô¨Çuid couplings between the bottle container and the decapsulator. Interconnects run inside TeÔ¨Çon containment tubing that can be fed from either side of the Bottle Box.
